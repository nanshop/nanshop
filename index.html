who we are

HEXXI is a place of a group of designers and architects with different backgrounds,
experience and professionalisms come together and have fun.

HEXXI develops projects in diverse areas by bringing together creative and art direction
with a wide range of clients, from cultural institutions and publishers to brands and private
companies, we work internationally but we focus locally

HEXXI interprets projects in a humanistic way as the co-ordinated action of people and
disciplines in the conviction that creativity is a shared and pluralist act.

HEXXI is a place open to cultural events and to collaboration with artists and professionals
working in all related fields.

focus

We study and research for cultural issues with tools through photography, documentary,
self-publish photo books, zines and digital media.
We also focus in a wide field of design and creative projects, as well as consultancy for
concept design and strategic design for visual improvements, we provide ongoing support
for digital media, feature additions and enhancements of the creative content.

If you are:
Local audiences who need cultural input! Artists! Designers! Manufacturers!
Photographers! Authors! Speakers! Entrepreneurs and freelancers! Small to medium-sized
businesses related to art and design! Art galleries and cultural institutions!
Talk to our team of designers, architects and communication specialists that crafts and
evolves meaningful cultural projects through effective ideas and strategic thinking.
