who we are

HEXXI is a place of a group of designers and architects with different backgrounds, experience and professionalisms come together and have fun.

HEXXI develops projects in diverse areas by bringing together creative and art direction with a wide range of clients, from cultural institutions and publishers to brands and private companies, we work internationally but we focus locally

HEXXI interprets projects in a humanistic way as the co-ordinated action of people and disciplines in the conviction that creativity is a shared and pluralist act.

HEXXI is a place open to cultural events and to collaboration with artists and professionals working in all related fields.

focus

We study and research for cultural issues with tools through photography, documentary,self-publish photo books, zines and digital media. We also focus in a wide field of design and creative projects,as well as consultancy for concept design and strategic design for visual improvements, we provide ongoing support for digital media, feature additions and enhancements of the creative content.
